Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

How do I make a tattoo enquiry?

If you are passing and we are open, then please feel free to come in and talk to us, or you can contact us by clicking one of the social media icons on the top of the page, or click HERE for other contact information.

Can I walk in for a tattoo or do I need to make an appointment?

You need to make an appointment for us to be able to discuss your requirements, so either pop in or contact us for a free no obligation consultation.

Do you have a long waiting period for appointments?

We are almost always fully booked at least 2 to 3 weeks in advance, so the quicker you can come for a consultation, the quicker we can make an appointment but we do sometimes have cancellations etc so check our facebook page by clicking HERE . Appointments already booked cannot be brought forward though.

What are your opening times?

Sun & Mon - closed

Tues to Fri 11.00 to 18.00

Sat - 12.00 to 18.00

Sundays and out of hour appointments can be arranged to suit you schedule.

What should I be aware of before booking a tattoo?

Click HERE to be redirected to a good website with some good advice about what to do before getting a tattoo, but here are some basics

You must be 18 or over and ID will be required on the day of your appointment.(We cannot accept parental consent for under 18’s).

We unfortunately cannot tattoo you if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.

If you have any medical conditions such as a blood borne virus etc, please make us aware of this during your consultation or before making your booking.

Do you take a deposit?

We take a 10% NON REFUNDABLE deposit at the time of booking an appointment

How should I prepare on the day for my tattoo?

A good breakfast is highly recommended, and too much alcohol the night before is not a good idea, as this can thin the blood.

Please be aware we will not tattoo anyone under the influence of drink or drugs.

We will shave the tattoo area for you, please do not do this yourself.

Can I see my design before my appointment?

Please feel free to stop by the studio if you wish to see or discuss your design before your appointment time

Time for small changes have been factored into your appointment time.

Can I use numbing cream?

We DO NOT recommend the use of numbing cream.

This can make the skin more difficult to tattoo and can sometimes affect how it heals.

There is often a greater level of pain experienced once it has worn off.

Are there any areas you will not tattoo?

The areas of the body which we do not tattoo are the sides of the fingers/palms, the inner lip and the soles of the feet as these areas tend to be very difficult to heal.

We do not tattoo in white ink alone as this tends to turn an off-white yellowy colour over time.

Please be aware that our artists will not directly copy another artists design, however we are willing to take inspiration from certain pieces and create your own original version for you..

What methods of payment do you accept?

To keep our prices as low as possible, we only accept cash payments.

Can I bring a friend or partner with me?

We politely ask that friends/partners are limited to 1 only during your session.

Our studio is small therefore your friend/partner must take care not to move around the space or lean on the tattoo chairs. This can be distracting for the artist at work.

Under 18’s, babies, dogs and bicycles are not permitted in the studio.

Thank you for your understanding

Are you a licensed studio and do you have a good needle disposable procedure in place?

Yes, we are a fully licenced studio and pride ourselves on having excellent levels of cleanliness. All of our needles are one-use disposables and we follow strict health and safety guidelines.

We use certified medical waste and sharps disposal contractors.

What type of ink do you use and it is vegan friendly?

We use Dynamic black, Intenze, and Eternal ink and these brands are all vegan friendly.

Do you do piercings?

We do not do piercings.

What type of tattooing do you offer?

All styles undertaken. Colour, B&G, traditional, custom, Old school, Neo traditional, Trash polka.

Do you do laser removal?

No we do not do laser removal

Do you do cover ups?

We do cover-ups, this can be discussed during the initial consultation.

What will happen to my tattoo if I lose or gain weight?

Your tattoo will only ever expand or reduce at the same rate as your skin, it should not distort in shape.

When can I exercise after having my new tattoo?

Exercise is fine, as long as the area is not knocked. Contact sports e.g Rugby are generally not advised until fully healed, due to the risk of knocking off scabs. swimming is not advised for at least 3 weeks.

After exercise make sure you wash the area with warm water and PH balance soap

Is it safe to swim and sun bathe after having a new tattoo?

We recommend at least 3 weeks before exposing a fresh tattoo to sun, sea or swimming, due to the risk of poor healing and infection.

It is always best to wait until after a holiday before having a new tattoo

How do I care for my new tattoo?

We will explain to you the after care a new tattoo needs, but as a general rule

Wash your tattoo with clean hands, using warm soapy water.

Rinse the area with cold water and pat dry.

Normal showering is fine just do not soak your tattoo or rub with a towel.

Apply Aveeno cream 2-3 times a day until your tattoo is healed.

Do not allow your tattoo to dry out.

Your tattoo will scab whilst it is healing, do not pick at the flakes as this can cause the colour to be pulled out.

Avoid swimming or suntanning until your tattoo has healed.

Apply a high SPF sunblock for the life of your tattoo to prevent fading.
